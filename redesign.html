How I redesigned the site

The Navbar


For the Navbar I used a combonation of the logo/title based template, the expandable menu template, and the verticle menu template. Truthfully the way it works was unexpected but not entirely unwelcome, I was more just playing around with formatting and ended up with something I liked. It also tracks nicely onto my original portfolio website.

The Homepage


For the homepage I used a series of padded grids with a container on top that acts as the title card. This was the basis of the majority of the site as well because of its high level of scalabilty. I then also used percentage based padding in order to further increase scalabilty with every page of the website scaling well across multiple devices. As for content, this was a carbon copy of my other portfolio website.

Resume


The resume is just a png with some auto scaling CSS.

Portfolio


The portfolio is an inverted grid based on the homepage that is also supposed to be a carbon copy of my other website. It utilizes the similar percentage based responsive web design as the homepage as well.

Contact Information


The contact information is suuper simple because I perfer that contect to be loged in the footer but because it had to be a separate page I just threw together another grid with some text in it.

Inspiration


For the inpiration page I used the accordion, I liked the way it matched onto the task of explaining what websites inspired me.
